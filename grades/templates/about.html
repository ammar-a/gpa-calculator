Welcome to tGPA!

Use this tool to predict your future gpa / set goals for the upcoming semester! You can also use it to calculate your current gpa if it has not been officially posted yet.

When you click 'calculate', tGPA will automatically save your marks, so that you can come back weeks or months later and redo your calculations without re-entering all your marks!

Tip: use the up and down arrow keys while entering number of courses to quickly add or remove rows.

Note: this is not an official tool for UofT, Ryerson or York.
